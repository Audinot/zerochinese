## Grammar  

**Interrogative pronoun 什麼**

The interrogative pronoun 什麼 is used in interrogative sentences, serving as the object by itself or together with a nominal element following it.

1. 你叫什麼名字？
2. 這是什麼？
3. 這是什麼書？

**The 是 Sentence**

A 是 sentence is a determinative sentence with 是, indicating what somebody or something equals or belongs to. The negative sentence is formed by adding the negative adverb 不 before 是.

| Subject | Verb | Noun        |
|---------|------|-------------|
| 李月    | 是   | 老師。      |
| 我      | 是   | 美國人。    |
| 我      | 不是 | 老師。      |

**Interrogative sentences with 嗎**

The particle 嗎 indicates an interrogative mood. When 嗎 is added at the end of a declarative sentence, the sentence turns into a question.
 
| Subject | Verb | Noun/Phrase | 嗎？ |
|---------|------|-------------|------|
| 你      | 是   | 美國人      | 嗎？ |
| 你      | 是   | 中國人      | 嗎？ |
| 你      | 是   | 老師        | 嗎？ |
