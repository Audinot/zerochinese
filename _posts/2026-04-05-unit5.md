### Grammar

**The interrogative pronoun 幾**

幾 is used to ask about a number, usually less than 10.

1. 你有幾個漢語老師？
2. 李老師家有幾口人？
3. 你女兒幾歲了？

**Indicating a change with 了**

了 is used at the end of a sentence to indicate a change, 
or the occurance of a new situation.

1. 李老師今年50歲了。
2. 我朋友的女兒今年四歲了。
3. 你女兒幾歲了？

**The interrogative phrase 多+大**

多大 is used to ask about one's age.

1. 你多大了？
2. 你女兒今年多大了？
3. 李老師今年多大了？
